Who are we?

How do we make our products?

  1. Selecting Quality Ingredients: We source the finest wheat from local farms in the Brasov region, ensuring that only the best grains are used in our bread-making process.
  2. Milling the Wheat: The harvested wheat is then milled in-house to produce the freshest flour possible, preserving its natural flavor and nutritional value
  3. Crafting the Dough: Our skilled bakers combine the freshly milled flour with water, salt, and yeast to create the dough, which is kneaded by hand to develop its texture and structure.
  4. Baking with Care: The dough is then shaped and allowed to rise before being baked in our wood-fired ovens, imparting a distinctive flavor and crust to the bread.
  5. Delivering Freshness: Once baked to perfection, our bread is ready to be enjoyed. We take pride in delivering our bread fresh from the Brasov farms.

From farm to table, our bread is a testament to our commitment to quality and tradition. Enjoy the taste of freshness with every slice from our bakery.
